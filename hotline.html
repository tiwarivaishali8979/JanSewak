What is Legal Hotline?

A legal hotline is a service that provides individuals with access to legal information and advice over the phone. It is typically staffed by attorneys or legal professionals who can offer guidance on various legal issues. Legal hotlines are often operated by nonprofit organizations, government agencies, or bar associations. People can call a legal hotline to get information about their rights, ask questions about legal processes, and seek general advice on legal matters. However, it's important to note that the assistance provided by legal hotlines is usually limited to general information and guidance, and they may not represent individuals in court.
